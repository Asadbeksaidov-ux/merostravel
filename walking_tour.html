Program Highlights

1

Mukhammad Aminkhan Madrasah & Minaret

The iconic unfinished blue tower (Kalta Minor) and the largest medieval madrasah in Khiva.

2

Kunya Ark Palace

The ancient citadel and residence of the Khans, offering panoramic views of the city.

3

Mukhammad Rahimkhan Madrasah

A historical center of education and poetry, facing the Kunya Ark.

4

Ak-Mechet Mosque

A unique small "White Mosque" with a distinctive conical dome and deep spiritual history.

5

Khiva Silk Carpet Workshop

Witness the traditional silk weaving process and see masterpieces being created by hand.

6

Pakhlavan Makhmud Mausoleum

The sacred heart of Ichan-Kala with stunning mesmerizing turquoise tiles.

7

Islam-Khodja Madrasah & Minaret

The tallest minaret in Khiva giving a spectacular skyline view.

8

Juma Mosque

The unique Friday Mosque with 213 ancient carved wooden columns.

9

Mukhammad Amin Inaq Madrasah

One of the earliest buildings in the eastern part of the inner city.

10

Tash-Khovli Palace

The "Stone Palace" known for its intricate Harem, lavish courtyards, and blue tiles.

11

Caravan Sarai

Explore the trading heritage of the Great Silk Road (Allakuli Khan complex).
